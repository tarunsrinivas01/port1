Practice
& Philosophy

Bhavana Studio is a Hyderabad-based interior design practice working across residential, hospitality, and commercial spaces. Every project begins with a single question: how should this space make you feel?

Deeply rooted in Indian craft traditions while fluent in contemporary spatial language, the studio brings together skilled artisans, material specialists, and light designers to create environments that are beautiful, liveable, and honest about where they come from.
